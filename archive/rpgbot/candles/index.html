Cantrip Candles are purveyors of magical scents, bringing immersion and ambience to tabletop roleplaying games across the realm since 2017. Our fantasy-themed fragrances are designed to enhance, inspire, and elevate your tabletop experience.

Located in the heart of Hollywood, our workshop serves as a manufacturing space, retail space, and gaming hub. Smell our candles in person and talk with our friendly staff about your tabletop campaign.

We're confident we can find the perfect scent to match your adventure!

Classes & Events

From our fan-favorite Candle Making Classes to exciting D&D campaigns and one-shot game nights for players of all skill levels, there's always something magical happening at our Los Angeles workshop. Select an upcoming event in the calendar to learn more, and register to join a game or class.

CLASS

Candle Making Classes

Our Candle Making Class is a unique, educational, in-person experience that allows you to create a custom scent inspired by your favorite tabletop adventure, player character, or whatever your heart desires. No prior experience is required and group discounts are available!

Book a Candle Making Class
CAMPAIGN

Chronicles of Cantrip

Join the roster of champions and adventurers vying to influence the ream as time flies by in this long-stretching saga. Each session takes place over a season and is a different scenario, putting you in the middle of the action every week. This is a campaign that prioritizes history made at the table over your characters backstory. Join one of the three various factions as they vye for power and influence, compete with other players for dominion over regions, and leave a lasting legacy.
