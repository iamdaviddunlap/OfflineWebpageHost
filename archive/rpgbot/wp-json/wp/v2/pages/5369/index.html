Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

The Witch’s patron is much like the Sorcerer’s bloodline: it adds new spells\n\twhich may not otherwise be available to the Witch, and can define a major\n\tportion of the Witch’s spell list.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

Many patrons provide one or more Polymorph spells. Polymorph is not normally\n\ton the Witch spell list, and polymorphing is something that you need to be\n\tbuilt for if you want to be good at it. See my\n\tPractical Guide to Polymorph<\/a>\n\tfor suggestions.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

    Agility<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

    The Agility domain provides some good buffs and a splash of polymorph. The\n\tspells are a great selection, and provide some excellent options.\n\tUnfortunately, because you only get a few polymorph spells, its hard to\n\tjustify devoting your character to polymorphing with this archetype.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    • jump<\/span>: Very situational. Not on the Witch\n\t\tspell list.<\/li\n\t>\n\t
    • cat’s grace<\/span>: A useful buff, but many\n\t\tcharacters who need dexterity will invest in items which provide it.<\/li\n\t>\n\t
    • haste<\/span>: One of the best buffs in the game.\n\t\tNot on the Cleric spell list.<\/li\n\t>\n\t
    • freedom of movement<\/span>: Excellent buff, but\n\t\tsituational.<\/li\n\t>\n\t
    • polymorph<\/span>: Extremely versatile, but don’t\n\t\tplan to use it on yourself often unless you built for polymorph.<\/li\n\t>\n\t

      Ancestors<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

      • bless<\/span>: A solid low-level buff. If you\n\t\tdon’t have any other morale bonuses available, this stays good forever.<\/li\n\t>\n\t
      • aid<\/span>: Mostly useful for the temporary\n\t\thitpoints.<\/li\n\t>\n\t
      • prayer<\/span>: The bonus is a luck bonus, so it\n\t\tstacks with Bless, and Prayer simultaneously applies a penalty to\n\t\tenemies.<\/li\n\t>\n\t
      • blessing of fervor<\/span>: Almost as good as\n\t\tHaste, but allows your allies to choose different bonuses every round.<\/li\n\t>\n\t
      • commune<\/span>: Expensive, but very powerful.<\/li\n\t>\n\t
      • greater heroism<\/span>: Great if only one person\n\t\tin your party needs buffs to their attack rolls. Doesn’t stack with\n\t\tBless.<\/li\n\t>\n\t
      • refuge<\/span>: Situational and very expensive.<\/li\n\t>\n\t
      • euphoric tranquility<\/span>: Like a high level\n\t\tversion of Charm Monster.<\/li\n\t>\n\t
      • weird<\/span>: Excellent AOE save or die, and if\n\t\tthey succeed on the save they are still stunned and take strength\n\t\tdamage.<\/li\n\t>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n
