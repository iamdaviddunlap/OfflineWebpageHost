Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

The Bloodrager is a weird fusion of the Barbarian and the Sorcerer. With full BAB, medium armor, a Rage mechanic similar to the Barbarian, and a small complement of spells, the Bloodrager is an excellent front-line Striker or Defender. Despite depending on Charisma for spells, the Bloodrager’s skill list doesn’t support the Bloodrager serving as a Face.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n
