Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

Do you ever want to be such a huge nerd that you could just stab your enemies with sheer big brain energy? Well Soulknife is at least half of that fantasy because for some reason you still use Dexterity to swing your Psychic damage brain blades. Probably balance reasons.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

This guide will show you all about the tricks to get the most out of the Soulknife subclass. You\u2019re not just a Psychic Sneak Attack dispenser. I mean, you\u2019re still that, one of the best for doing it at range even, but you\u2019re much more than just that.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

As a Soulknife, we have all the fun combat tricks that you think of with a Rogue: sneak attack for piles of d6’s, dart in and out of combat on a whim, sneak around unseen. What we have on top of all the goodness that is Rogue is a reliable pool of bonus dice that can be used to add to rolls and change outcomes to our advantage, from skill checks to attack rolls, as well as spending the dice on unique utility powers.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

              Optional Class Features<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
              <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

              Tasha\u2019s introduced optional features for the base classes. Some classes received more than other classes, but Rogue received a single ability at level 3, Steady Aim. This is a great tool for us as a Soulknife because it requires standing still to gain Advantage and we can simply throw our Psychic Blade as a ranged attack. We have to give up the off-hand attack, but that\u2019s a fair trade if aiming gives us Sneak Attack.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

              If for some reason you\u2019re not able to use this feature, it\u2019s not the end of the world. We still have plenty of uses for the bonus action. Again, you could simply use the bonus action second psychic dagger. Mathematically, this is the same odds as aiming to gain Advantage on one attack, with the bonus of getting more damage if both rolls are good.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

              The drawback is that we can\u2019t just have Advantage for a free Sneak Attack. Or can we? With Cunning Action, we can Hide as a Bonus Action, which is not completely free and we do need some way to actually Hide, but assuming we can have the right conditions we\u2019ll gain Advantage.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

              Soulknife Ability Scores<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
              <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

              The thing about being a skill monkey is that you have some options here. Dexterity will be your highest stat here of course, since that’s your combat effectiveness. I would advise against dumping Constitution because dying is suboptimal.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

              After that, we look at your party and see what kinds of skills we\u2019re lacking. If you need to be the face and pick up those pickup lines, Charisma. If you need to be the Scholar, then big-brain that Intelligence score. If you don\u2019t like surprises, Wisdom will ruin all those nice parties your DM is planning to throw for you.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n
