Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

In 5e D&D, Skill Proficiencies represent an abstract set of knowledge that can be applied to ability checks. Tool Proficiencies are the same way, but tend to be overlooked and glossed over because of their supposedly narrow focus. In this guide, we\u2019ll be going over the majority of tools to find some interesting uses, like an expansion to the idea put forth in Xanathar\u2019s Guide to Everything<\/a> starting on page 78. Particularly, Synergies grant Advantage when two related proficiencies overlap, and this is by no means an exhaustive list of such bonuses. You should instead use this as a jumping off point for even more creative uses for combining tools.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

If you\u2019re familiar with previous editions, particularly D&D 3.5 and PF1e, you might notice that tools serve a similar function to Craft, Profession, and some Knowledge skills. The great part about this change is that in 5e it\u2019s easy to get an extra tool proficiency or two to round out your character for roleplay purposes. Previously, these skills had to compete with important core skills like Spot or Search and it was a major investment to be a Carpenter, for example.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            Artisan\u2019s Tools<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
            <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

            These tools are primarily concerned with making and repairing things. Proficiency with an Artisan\u2019s Tool is both knowledge of the craft in question as well as the workings of the related profession. This includes the function of another skill from previous editions: Appraising the value of an object from your craft.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Item Crafting<\/strong> – Artisan\u2019s tools can be used to craft all manner of things, mundane and magical. This takes time based on the price of the item, or the rarity of the magic item, a week per 50gp for mundane items, but the time can be shortened with multiple craftspersons working together, dividing the total time per crafter. For more detailed and specific information, consult XGtE<\/a> page 128. For healing potions, see Herbailsm Kit below.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Work<\/strong> – As a downtime activity, Artisans\u2019 Tools and Musical Instruments can both be used to earn a wage during downtime. For results, check page 134 of XGtE<\/a>.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            Alchemist\u2019s Supplies<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
            <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

            Alchemy is just chemistry, and this proficiency gives a character knowledge of chemistry as well as other related sciences. To that end, if Firearms are allowed in your game this tool would also allow the creation of gunpowder.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Alchemical Crafting<\/strong> – This proficiency allows for the crafting of alchemical items by using materials worth half the price of purchasing the item. <\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Jeweller\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – Together, these proficiencies are the foundation of metallurgy. Deeper analysis of metals and their composition can be performed when one understands both of these disciplines.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Smith\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – Creating ammunition for firearms is much easier if one understands both Smithing and Alchemical processes.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Brewer\u2019s Supplies<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
            <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

            The basic use of Brewer\u2019s Supplies is the making of beers and other liquors. While this doesn\u2019t automatically confer a deep understanding of microbiology, a knowledgeable Brewer will still be familiar with the concept of Yeast.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Potable Water<\/strong> –  Brewing equipment can be used to filter and sanitize water, making it safe to drink.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Alchemist\u2019s Supplies<\/strong> – Alchemical processes grant better insight into concepts of distillation to create not just hard liquors but also incredible flammable liquids.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Cook\u2019s Utensils<\/strong> – Turning alcohol into vinegars on purpose is a fantastic way to prevent food spoilage before a long journey or a harsh winter.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Herbalism Kit<\/strong> – Knowing how to brew plants into drinks can be taken a few steps further to extracting and distilling all manner of liquid drugs, both recreational and medicinal.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Calligrapher\u2019s Supplies<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
            <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

            Calligraphy is about elevating words and writing to artwork. Anyone can put pen to page to scribble down a message but a Calligrapher knows how to catch the eye.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Decipher Treasure Map<\/strong> – These tools give insight into finding secrets about a map, such as the age or hidden messages.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Woodcarver\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – Using the beautiful lettering as a carving stencil is one way to really elevate both arts.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Carpenter\u2019s Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
            <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

            Carpentry is about making solid wooden objects. This familiarity with construction techniques helps in assessing when something is built in an unusual manner or where a structure might be vulnerable, whether your intent is demolition or reinforcement.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Fortify<\/strong> – With a little work, you can make a door or window harder to break down.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Temporary Shelter<\/strong> – Hastily construct a dry and shady place for resting.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Mason\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – Wooden molds are integral for pouring concrete mixtures for more improved construction tasks.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Vehicles<\/strong> – While Vehicle proficiencies include knowledge of maintenance, putting these skills together grant insight into making and installing improvements to the vehicles beyond simple maintenance.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Cartographer\u2019s Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n

            While anyone can jot down a rough map with basic relative locations, a Cartographer knows how to draw a proper map for accurate navigation and surveying.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Craft a Map<\/strong> – Make a map while travelling in addition to other activities.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Carpenter\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – An architectural draft is just a very specific kind of map and pairing up these fields of expertise will help you get a birds\u2019 eye view of a structure from the inside. This benefit also applies with Mason\u2019s Tools<\/strong> in a stone or brick structure.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Navigator\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – Understanding the practices of navigation by stars will allow one to create star charts, particularly handy if Spelljamming to an unfamiliar Crystal Sphere.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Cobbler\u2019s Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
            <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

            Boots, shoes, and all manner of hard-bottomed footwear are the domain of the Cobbler.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Maintain Shoes<\/strong> – Travel farther on foot without fatigue.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Craft Hidden Compartment<\/strong> – With some work, add hidden storage to some shoes.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Animal Handling<\/strong> – The shoeing of hooved animals such as horses is similar enough in principle to cobbling that cross-training such as this helps not only with the installation but also the diagnosis of any maladies that might afflict the hooves. Creating the horseshoes however is a job for Smith\u2019s Tools<\/strong>.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Smith\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – Could you imagine how painful greaves would be if they were made entirely from metal? Having the skill to assemble sturdy yet comfortable boot soles as a base for any sort of foot protection definitely makes things easier.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Cook\u2019s Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
            <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

            Making food edible is not the same as turning ingredients into a meal. These tools are far too often overlooked by adventurers doing everything in their power to get out of needing to eat, forgetting that there is a noticable difference between not feeling hungry and feeling satisfied.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Prepare Meals<\/strong> – Well cooked meals grant extra healing during short rests.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Animal Handling<\/strong> – Animals have a wide range of diets and while some forms of feed are certainly trail stable, more exotic diets might benefit from preparation to preserve freshness and prevent spoilage without making the food unpalatable to your animal companions.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Arcana<\/strong> – With access to Prestidigitation a magically inclined Chef can create seemingly impossible flavor combinations.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Poisoner\u2019s Kit<\/strong> – A particularly devious poisoner can mask the taste by choosing the right legitimate food ingredients.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Vehicles (Water)<\/strong> – On a long voyage, the food needs to be resistant to spoilage and remain edible for months at a time, so special techniques and considerations must be made to keep morale up with the limited available ingredients.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Glassblower\u2019s Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
            <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

            A glassblower is knowledgeable in both the art of shaping glass as well as the making of different kinds of glass.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Identify Weakness<\/strong> – Find the right spot to hit a glass object when trying to break it.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Jeweller\u2019s Kit<\/strong> – With the right tools, the same methods of facetting and setting jewels can be used to grind and mount lenses.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Smith\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – Stained glass artwork is a combination of wrought metal (commonly lead) and glass.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Jeweller\u2019s Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
            <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

            Turning rough stones into beautifully faceted gems and shaping precious metals to hold said gems is the craft of Jewellers.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Identify Gems<\/strong> – Appraise gems at a glance.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Disguise Kit<\/strong> – Adding some real jewels to enhance your costume will help you pass scrutiny.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Leatherworker\u2019s Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
            <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

            These are the tools for skinning game animals, preparing hides into leather, and turning that leather into finished products.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Identify Hides<\/strong> – With some quick study, you can identify the source of leathers and hides, such as what crafting techniques were used to assemble a piece.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Animal Handling<\/strong> – Putting in a little work to custom fit a saddle and bridle to your mount can really make a difference when it counts.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Vehicles (Land)<\/strong> – Similar to Animal Handling<\/strong>, maintaining and fitting your pack animals bits and bridles can make it easier to keep control in adverse conditions.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Mason\u2019s Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
            <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

            Masonry is all about brick and stone based construction. Knowing how things are built offers vital insight into how they might be demolished as well.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Demolition<\/strong> – You deal double damage to stone and brick structures.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Smith\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – Reinforcing a stone structure with rebar during construction allows for larger and more stable construction.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Painter\u2019s Supplies<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
            <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

            Both for making artwork as well as simply painting a structure such as a fence.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Painting and Drawing<\/strong> – Make art during rests. Copy down a rough image of a scene or a copy of some art.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Tinker\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – Normally after a repair job it\u2019s easy to identify that something has been repaired, but with some painting skill, color matching the replaced parts is much easier.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Potter\u2019s Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n

            The methods of shaping and mixing clays and ceramics are invaluable for the making of pottery as well as identifying the original form of a broken clay object.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Reconstruction<\/strong> – Use forensic techniques to reconstruct the original shape of pottery from shards.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Glassblowers Tools<\/strong> – Glass inlays on ceramics can elevate the quality of the artwork. Similar effects with gems can be done with a Jeweller\u2019s Kit<\/strong> or metals with Smith\u2019s Tools<\/strong>.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n

            Smith\u2019s Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n

            A smith is knowledgeable in techniques of smelting raw ores into workable metals as well as the working of said metals into useful objects, such as weapons or armor. With cross-discipline skills metalworking can improve the durability of many other crafted items.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Repair<\/strong> – Repair damaged metal objects.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Alchemist\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – Deeper understanding of metals and chemical reactions are the key to smelting powerful alloys and extracting more exotic elements from rare ores.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Carpenter\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – Knowledge of construction and metalworking combine to build metal framed buildings.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Vehicles (Water)<\/strong> – Accounting for the constant jostling of the waves is paramount when building or repairing cannons for ship to ship combat.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Tinker\u2019s Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n

            Tinker\u2019s Tools are unusual. Unlike most Artisan\u2019s tools, they aren’t used to really craft anything but are rather an all-purpose quick repair and patch kit. As such they have a more general synergy with every tool.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Repair<\/strong> – Perform repairs on a multitude of objects.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Any Tool<\/strong> – When rushing a patch job, having knowledge of both Tinker\u2019s Tools and a relevant proficiency, such as Carpenter\u2019s Tools<\/strong> for a wooden object or Cobbler\u2019s Tools<\/strong> for shoes, offsets any penalties for rushing the repairs.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Weaver\u2019s Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n

            Whether spinning thread or sewing clothes, these are the tools you\u2019ll need to learn.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Repair<\/strong> – Repair cloth objects.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Craft Clothing<\/strong> – Make an outfit during a long rest.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Animal Handling<\/strong> – Know what fur and hair make good threads helps in getting a good yield while shearing animals.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Woodcarver\u2019s Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n

            While Carpentry is about assembling wood into structures like cabinets, Woodcarving is about making intricate designs or precise cuts into a wooden surface. Additionally Woodcarving is used for making arrows and bolts.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Craft Arrows<\/strong> – Craft wooden ammunition, including arrows, bolts, and javelins, as part of a rest.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Carpenter\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – Additional flourishes and other intricate design work increase the aesthetic quality of your products.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Assorted Tools<\/h2>\n\n\n

            The rest of the tools don’t have a common category like Artisans’ Tools, but that is fine. Many of these replace other forgotten skills from editions past. In the interest of removing redundancy, Instruments and Gaming Sets are going to be condensed into their categories.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Disguise Kit<\/h3>\n\n\n

            Unsurprisingly, this replaced the Disguise skill. While the obvious benefit of making a mundane disguise is the primary purpose of this tool, this kit also covers the broader idea of costuming and makeup artistry in general.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Create Disguise<\/strong> – Assemble a disguise as part of a long rest.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Musical Instruments<\/strong> – Enhancing your act with appropriate style choices will help get the crowd on your side.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Forgery Kit<\/h3>\n\n\n

            Would you be surprised at this point if I told you that Forgery was the skill in 3.5 that this replaces? Forgery is about making convincing duplicate objects. This is not limited to documents like some might reasonably assume, but anything one could make could be forged.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Quick Fake<\/strong> – Forge documents of varying lengths as part of a short or long rest.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Artisan\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – The best way to fake the work of a master craftsperson is to be<\/em> a master craftsperson.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Musical Instruments<\/strong> – Knowing music theory would make forged sheet music far more convincing if you needed to do that for some reason.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Gaming Sets<\/h3>\n\n\n

            Games of chance and skill are all interchangeable as far as what they can do, but the differ in what kind of ability check they work with. Playing cards for example might be Wisdom to read your opponent without giving up anything in return, while Dragonchess is instead likely played with Intelligence checks.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Gambling<\/strong> – As a downtime activity, you can spend a week gambling. Details are on page 130 of XGtE<\/a>.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Brewer\u2019s Supplies<\/strong> – Bringing your strongest drinks to the game might be the edge you need to get your opponent to slip up.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Herbalism Kit<\/h3>\n\n\n

            It\u2019s strange that this isn\u2019t considered an Artisan\u2019s Tool because the primary use for most adventurers is to brew healing potions. It\u2019s also used for identifying plants which some might consider a Nature skill check as well.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Brew Healing Potions<\/strong> – This proficiency allows for crafting of healing potions. While there are four rarities of potion, anything beyond the standard potion of healing is overpriced and takes far too long to craft.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Identify Plants<\/strong> – Quickly identify plants.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Calligrapher\u2019s Supplies<\/strong> – Exotic dyes made from plants add that little extra to make your writing pop.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Forgery Kit<\/strong> – Other strange and wonderful plants can create wondrous yet mundane inks that vanish while remaining hidden from magical scrutiny.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Musical Instruments<\/h3>\n\n\n

            Similar to Gaming sets, the things that you can accomplish with instruments are roughly interchangeable, but while a Flute might call for a Dexterity check, holding a long note to sound an alarm with a Horn is likely to be a Constitution check.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Compose a Tune<\/strong> – Write a new song over a long rest.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Tinker\u2019s Tools<\/strong> – It\u2019s far easier to repair an instrument if you can play it to check how the repairs are going.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Navigator\u2019s Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n

            While Cartographer\u2019s tools are for making a map, Navigator\u2019s tools are for reading those maps. Looking to the past, the 3.5 Knowledge skills Local and Geography have been combined into this tool.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Sighting<\/strong> – Locate your position on a chart and determine time of day with careful measurements.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Performance<\/strong> – By turning directions into a catchy song it will be easier for others to recall and follow them.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Poisoner\u2019s Kit<\/h3>\n\n\n

            Like the Herbalism kit except angry. No really, it covers most of the same plant knowledge as the Herbalism Kit but focuses on dangerous plants instead of helpful ones.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Handle Poison<\/strong> – Never risk poisoning yourself while using poisons.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Calligrapher\u2019s Supplies<\/strong> – Mix a deadly contact poison into the ink and spread death via mail.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Herbalism Kit – For many substances, the difference between poison and cure is quantity.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Thieves\u2019 Tools<\/h3>\n\n\n

            For being the most prominently used tool in the game, it\u2019s funny that alphabetical order put it almost dead last. This tool is the new face of Open Lock and Disable Device skills from 3.5 and it\u2019s rather useful for dungeoneering.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Set a Trap<\/strong> –  Assemble a trap as part of a short rest.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Synergies<\/h4>\n\n\n

            Poisoner\u2019s Kit<\/strong> – Salvaging the poison off of a disabled trap for later is technically recycling and therefore good for the dungeon environment.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Vehicles<\/h2>\n\n\n

            Classified as Tools because they don\u2019t fit anywhere else, Land and Water Vehicles are an incredibly broad category. In the majority of campaigns, this is fine because Vehicles are an unusual thing to encounter or need. In Naval or Siege Warfare campaigns however, it might be prudent to subdivide these proficiencies into more specialized roles.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

            Vehicle Handling<\/strong> – When piloting, your Proficiency Bonus applies to the vehicle\u2019s AC and Saves.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Land<\/h3>\n\n\n

            Land vehicles encompass a wide array of wheeled or sledding vehicles that are usually drawn by beasts of burden. In 3.5, Knowledge Architecture and Engineering, Profession Siege Engineer, or Handle Animal were the closest relevant skills.<\/p>\n\n\n

            Siege Warfare Subdivisions<\/h4>\n\n\n
