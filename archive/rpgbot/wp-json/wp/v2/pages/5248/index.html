Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

The Gunslinger is a Striker, plain and simple. If your favorite trick is “use gun on man”, the Gunslinger is for you. With a bit of optimization the Gunslinger is easy to play and produces a reliable stream of damage against nearly any enemy.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

        Gunslinger Class Features<\/h2>\n\n\n

        Hit Points<\/span>: d10 hit points is plenty,\n\tespecially because gunslingers are almost exclusively ranged combatants.<\/p>\n

        Base Attack Bonus<\/span>: Full BAB.<\/p>\n

        Saves<\/span>: Good Fortitude and Reflex saves, and\n\twith enough Wisdom to power the Grit pool, and the Gunslinger’s saves are very\n\tgood.<\/p>\n

        Proficiencies<\/span>: Only light armor, but access\n\tto martial weapons. Oh, and firearms. Don’t forget those.<\/p>\n

        Skills<\/span>: 4+ skill ranks, but the\n\tGunslinger’s skill list contains very few options that work for the\n\tGunslinger.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

        <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

        Gunsmith<\/span>: Gunsmithing allows you to\n\tmaintain you weapon, craft new ones, and craft your own ammunition. Getting a\n\tfree gun at first level saves you a whole pile of money, and being able to\n\tupgrade it for 300 gp saves you the cost of buying or crafting a new one when\n\tyou’re ready to upgrade to masterwork.<\/p>\n

        Grit<\/span> (Ex): Grit fuels the Gunglinger’s\n\tdeeds, but it’s a very limited pool. You can restore grit with critical hits\n\t(very difficult) or with killing blows (much less difficult), but you still\n\twant to use Grit sparingly.<\/p>\n

        Deeds<\/span>: Gunslinger deeds offer numerous\n\ttactical options for the gunslinger. See my\n\tGunslinger Deeds Breakdown<\/a>\n\tfor more help with Gunslinger Deeds.<\/p>\n

        Nimble<\/span> (Ex): A nice dodge bonus to AC will\n\thelp make up for limiting the Gunslinger to medium armor, and is particularly\n\tuseful against other firearms. You can supplement this by fighting\n\tdefensively, by using Combat Expertise, and with the Halfling’s favored class\n\tbonus.<\/p>\n

        Bonus Feats<\/span>: The Gunslinger gets half as\n\tmany as the Fighter, but this is till a great source of additional feats.<\/p>\n

        Gun Training<\/span> (Ex): This is a huge boost in\n\tdamage output.<\/p>\n

        True Grit<\/span> (Ex): Many deeds cost a point of\n\tgrit to do something awesome, and being able to perform those deeds for free\n\tevery round can make the Gunslinger considerably more effective.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

        Abilities<\/h2>\n\n\n

        Dexterity defines the Gunslinger, but a bit of Wisdom is important for the Gunslinger’s Grit pool.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

        Str<\/span>: Dump to 7.<\/p>\n

        Dex<\/span>: Dexterity is everything for the\n\tGunslinger.<\/p>\n

        Con<\/span>: More hitpoints are always welcome, but\n\tthe Gunslinger already gets d10 hit points and good Fortitude saves.<\/p>\n

        Int<\/span>: The gunslinger’s skill list is pretty\n\tbad and they already get 4+ skill ranks.<\/p>\n

        Wis<\/span>: Crucial for the Grit pool and the\n\tGunslinger’s poor Will saves.<\/p>\n
