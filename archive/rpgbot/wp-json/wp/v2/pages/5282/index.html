Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

The monk has always been a difficult class to master. With an odd assortment of abilities which frequently don’t cooperate, the Monk has trouble being effective. Still, with the right archetype the Monk can be Defender, Scout, Striker, or even a Support or Librarian. For the most part, the monk serves best as a Defender or Striker, and the better Monk archetypes tend to reflect this.<\/p>\n\n\n
