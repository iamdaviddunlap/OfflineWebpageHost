Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

Arcane Trickster spells’ focus on Enchantment and Illusion spells solidifies\n\ttheir niche as tricky and deceptive, but largely locks them out of directly\n\toffensive or defensive spell options. While these options can often complement\n\tthe core rogue’s capabilities, they don’t replace them.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

This page applies almost exclusively to Arcane Tricksters. Other rogues who\n\ttake Magic Initiate may also find this section beneficial. Because Arcane\n\tTricksters are mostly limited to Enchantment and Illusion spells, I’ll focus\n\ton those spells here, but I will note some notable options for the total of 7\n\tspells which you can learn that go beyond the school limitation.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n
