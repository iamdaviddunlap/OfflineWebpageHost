Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

The Druid’s Nature Bond ability allows the selection of either an animal companion or a domain. Because you don’t get two domains like a Cleric, your choice of domain is very important. You still get bonus domain spells, but with only one domain you are stuck with whatever your domain provides at that level.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

Remember that domains which are good or bad for a Cleric may not be as good or bad for a Druid. Because their base spell lists are different, some domains provide Cleric spells to Druids, and others which provide lots of Druid spell to Clerics offer few new options to Druids.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n
