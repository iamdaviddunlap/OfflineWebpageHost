Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

The Arcanist is a fantastic new take of Arcane spellcasting. Arcanists prepare spells at the beginning of the day like a Wizard, then cast spells using daily slots like a Sorcerer. This makes the Arcanist versatile, powerful, and a great introduction for players who are new to spellcasting. Arcanist Exploits add a level of customization and introduce a lot of excellent options.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

Keep in mind that Arcanists need both Intelligence and<\/em> Charisma for their spells (for maximum spell level and bonus spells, and for spell DC, respectively), which makes them more MAD than a Wizard.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

        Arcanist Class Features<\/h2>\n\n\n

        Hit Points<\/span>: d6 hit points is the worst in the\n\tgame, so don’t go drawing fire.<\/p>\n

        Base Attack Bonus<\/span>: 1\/2 BAB. Fortunately, you\n\twon’t need it.<\/p>\n

        Saves<\/span>: Will is the Arcanist’s only good save,\n\tand with no real dependency on any ability score which contributes to saving\n\tthrows, Arcanists need to make a point to enhance their saves.<\/p>\n

        Proficiencies<\/span>: No armor, but access to all\n\tsimple weapons offers plenty of options.<\/p>\n

        Skills<\/span>: Despite receiving only 2+ skill\n\tranks, the Arcanist actually gets a lot of skills because of their dependency\n\ton Intelligence. Access to all Knowledge skills makes the Arcanist a good\n\tLibrarian, and access to Use Magic Device opens up a lot of interesting\n\toptions.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

        Spellbooks<\/span>: Identical to the Wizard class\n\tfeature.<\/p>\n

        Arcane Reservoir<\/span> (Su): Even without exploits\n\tto use your Arcane Reservoir, the ability to boost your caster level or DC\n\tgive you some fantastic options. Boost DCs for your save-or-suck spells, and\n\tboost caster level for damage spells or spells with short durations.<\/p>\n

        Abilities<\/h2>\n\n\n

        Like the Sorcerer and Wizard, the Arcanist is a primary caster, and is very SAD. Really all you need is Intelligence.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

        Str<\/span>: Dump to 7.<\/p>\n

        Dex<\/span>: Needed for saves and AC.<\/p>\n

        Con<\/span>: Needed for saves and HP.<\/p>\n

        Int<\/span>: All that you need to do your job.<\/p>\n

        Wis<\/span>: Needed for saving throws, but Will is\n\tyour good save, so don’t worry about it too much.<\/p>\n
