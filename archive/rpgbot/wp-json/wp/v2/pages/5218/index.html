    Archetypes<\/h2>\n\n\n

    The Bard is fantastically versatile. With a long skill list and 6+ skill points, he Bard can cover nearly every skill. His selection of spells isn’t as broad as the Wizard or the Cleric, but the Bard gets access to many fantastic support and area control spells, as well as a few Cure spells at low levels. With the right build and archetype, the Bard can fill any role in the party. The vanilla Bard is excels at Support and is a perfect Librarian, and can sometimes work as a Striker, Healer, or Controller depending on his spell selection.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
