Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

Tentacles and dark ocean depths are a Fathomless Warlock’s best friend. While it\u2019s certainly far more useful in an aquatic situation, the Fathomless can hold its own on land as well. So if you don\u2019t shy away from that which wriggles around in mucosal secretions, grab hold of my tentacle and take the plunge. We promise you\u2019re not allowed to drown. Not anymore.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

Silly intro aside, the Fathomless Warlock has a pseudopod pseudo-pet, the Tentacle of the Deeps, that can smack things and protect allies. Alongside that, Fathomless has a decent spell list with some fun options, including Black Tentacles. We\u2019ll discuss these features and more in full below.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

Our example build, Never Safe From Water, does some fun tricks by creating various zones of safety as well as harm, thanks to features like the Tentacle of the Deeps and Black Tentacles. By utilizing the Pact of the Talisman and its invocations, we, or our chosen partner, are never caught out in a sticky situation. Adventuring is a dangerous business after all; always wear protection.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            Fathomless Ability Scores<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
            <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

            We like all the same ability scores that other Warlocks like. High Charisma for casting and interpersonal skills, decent Dexterity for AC and flexibility, and Constitution for going all night long.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

            Str<\/span>: Of all the scores, this is the most likely to get dumped, as of the scores we don\u2019t need it has the least important Saves.<\/p>\n

            Dex<\/span>: We\u2019ll want at least some for AC purposes. While we don\u2019t naturally get Medium armor, if we can multiclass or grab Moderately Armored then a 14 is good enough.<\/p>\n

            Con<\/span>: This is for HP, Concentration, and saves that suck.<\/p>\n

            Int<\/span>: Of the dumpable stats, we can put leftover points here since Arcana is a useful skill for any caster.<\/p>\n

            Wis<\/span>: These saves suck to fail, but we have proficiency, so we can let this go.<\/p>\n

            Cha<\/span>: Our casting stat. Really important.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n
