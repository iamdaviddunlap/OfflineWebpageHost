    Battle Scout<\/a><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

    Class Skills<\/span>: The Beastmaster adds\n\tAcrobatics and Escape Artist, but loses Knowledge (Dungeoneering), Knowledge\n\t(Geography), Profession, and Spellcraft.     Animal Companion<\/span> (Ex): This opens up the\n\tfull animal companion list for rangers, which is nice since they get a fairly\n\tlimited list by default.     Improved Empathic Link<\/span> (Su): Seeing\n\tthrough your animal companion’s eyes is nice, or you could just have someone\n\tcast Clairvoyance. You also give up your 6th level combat feat.<\/p>\n

    Strong Bond<\/span> (Ex): And finally at level 12,\n\tyour animal companions catch up to the druid.       Ranger’s Luck<\/span> (Ex): Rerolls are always\n\tnice, and you get to use this multiple times per day as you level. Forcing\n\tenemies to reroll is also nice, as it effectively makes you resistant to\n\tcritical hits..<\/p>\n
