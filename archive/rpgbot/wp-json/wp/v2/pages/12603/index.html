Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

Originally designed as the depiction of the Azorius guild from one of the Magic the Gathering crossover books, the Order Domain Cleric provides an incredibly powerful feature that immediately drew the attention of optimizers everywhere. Once it was then added to a non-setting-specific sourcebook, it promptly became a valuable choice in the field of powerful Clerics.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

With a strong focus on \u201cmotivation\u201d in the form of making their allies attack out of turn and empowering their enchantment spells, this subclass provides many tools to be an incredibly effective Cleric. With that said, as you\u2019ll see below, it actually provides you even better tools to do something else entirely.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

            No change from standard cleric, with the caveat that you\u2019re one of the domains that gets free heavy armor. If you can get your str up to 15 to wear full plate without sacrificing too much, you should. With that said, all you really lose for wearing it anyway is 10 feet of movement speed which is way less important as a support caster than you might imagine. Especially if you spend early resources on a broom of flying which has its own speed and doesn\u2019t give a lick about what yours is.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
