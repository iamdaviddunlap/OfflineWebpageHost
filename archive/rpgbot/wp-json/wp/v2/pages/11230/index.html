Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

High level transmutation magic offers some of the most tempting and easily abusable magic available. True Polymorph didn’t exist before 5th edition and is an odd combination of Polymorph Any Object and Shapechange that has the notable distinction of being permanent if you can concentrate on it for the full hour. <\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

Now, in terms of power, neither True Polymorph nor 5e’s version of Shapechange hold a candle to Wish<\/a>. But sometimes you want to break the world in other interesting ways, and True Polymorph is a great way to do so. The whole spell is so vaguely worded that basically any casting of it starts with an argument with your DM, so I’m going to try and provide some clarity here.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n
