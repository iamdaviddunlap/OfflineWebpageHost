          Primal Companion Options<\/h5>\n\n\n\n

          Tasha’s Cauldron of Everything presents a replacement mechanic for the Beast Master’s Companion feature. Rather than selecting an animal with a published stat block, you pick from one of three generic beasts and you can describe it however you like.           Raising your companion from the dead now takes a spell slot (so long as you\n\tdo so within an hour of its death), allowing you throw your companion into\n\tcombat with little regard for its safey confident in the knowledge that you\n\tcan bring it right back to life good as new.                         Beast Master vs. Drakewarden<\/h4>\n\n\n\n
                        <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n
