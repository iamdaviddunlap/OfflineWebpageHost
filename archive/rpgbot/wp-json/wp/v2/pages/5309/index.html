Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

The Ranger is a very versatile class. Based on your combat style and skill selection, the ranger can serve as a tank, a scout, a striker, and a librarian to some degree. How you function in combat is defined largely by your combat style, but how you function as a character is more broadly defined by your archetype.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

See also: Ranger Archetypes Breakdown<\/a>.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

The most important part of the Ranger’s Combat Style feature is that you can select bonus feats without meeting the pre-requisites. Many combat styles grant the Ranger feats several levels before a normal character could take them.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n
