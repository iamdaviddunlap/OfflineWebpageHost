Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

The Warpriest is a combination of the Fighter and the Cleric, but manages to be distinctively different from the Paladin. The Warpriest depends more heavily on spells than the Paladin, and have very few defensive class abilities. Fervor allows the Warpriest to rely on self-buffing spells without cutting into damage output, and the Warpriest’s abilities are full of great buff options usable as a Swift Action.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

    Abilities<\/h2>\n\n\n
