Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

The Druid is among the most powerful and versatile classes in the game. With a pet Defender\/Striker, a diverse spell list, and the Wild Shape ability, the Druid can fill nearly any role in the party except the Face.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

The two biggest decision points for the Druid are Nature Bond (Animal Companion vs. Druid Domain) and Wild Shape. While Nature Bond is an either\/or decision, Wild Shape is a Yes\/No decision: Being good at polymorph effects like Wild Shape requires planning from level 1. If you plan to use Wild Shape, read my Practical Guide to Polymorph<\/a>.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n
