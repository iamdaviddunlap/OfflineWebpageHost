Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

The Samurai. A Title. A Class. A Profession. Just saying the word evokes certain ideas and images of what a Samurai is, what a Samurai does, and how a Samurai fights. Don\u2019t let those images restrict you. Samurai as a Fighter subclass is not bound by the name Samurai. Not that you can\u2019t make your Samurai just like the real-world namesake if you wanted to, that’s up to you.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

Mechanically, the Samurai is a powerhouse because of the ability to just decide \u201cI have Advantage this turn\u201d a few times a day. Taking advantage of your on-demand Advantage is key to playing Samurai and we\u2019ll discuss ways to do that as we go through options for the class.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

Our example build is not only a great way to show off the diverse options for building a Samurai, but an exercise in flavour as well. If you look at it and think \u201cHow is this a Samurai?\u201d then I\u2019ve done my job. If you look at it and think \u201cYeah that\u2019s exactly what a Samurai is.\u201d then you\u2019re in on the reference.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

          Samurai Ability Scores<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
          <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

          As a Fighter, we have options. We can either go with Strength or Dexterity as a primary attack stat and our subclass abilities work equally well with Melee and Ranged options. So pick whichever you want, leave the other behind and put the rest of your points into Constitution and Wisdom.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

          Str<\/span>: We can use this and go swinging with big heavy two-handed weapons.<\/p>\n

          Dex<\/span>: Or we can use this and shoot from afar while having finesse weapons to keep safe in melee.<\/p>\n

          Con<\/span>: Don\u2019t let this fall behind. Dying is suboptimal.<\/p>\n

          Int<\/span>: We\u2019re not the knowledge guy.<\/p>\n

          Wis<\/span>: We\u2019ll be getting Proficiency in these saves at level 7, so maybe use some leftover points at creation.<\/p>\n

          Cha<\/span>: We\u2019re going to need too many points in other places to really focus on this, but we do have some Face abilities so if you\u2019re rolling for stats and get a 4th good one, this might be a place for it.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n
