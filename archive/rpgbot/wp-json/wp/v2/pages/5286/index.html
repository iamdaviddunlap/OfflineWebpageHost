Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

The Ninja is an alternate class to the Rogue, combining some of the Monk’s mystic abilities with the Rogue’s talent for stealth, cunning, and stabbing things.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

          Abilities<\/h2>\n\n\n

          Ninjas primarily rely on Dexterity, but need Charisma for their Ki Pool and a splash of every other ability to keep themelves functional. They are somewhat MAD, but really only need a splash of their less important abilities..<\/p>\n\n\n\n

          Str<\/span>: Helpful for melee and thrown weapon\n\tdamage, but otherwise useless.<\/p>\n

          Dex<\/span>: Your primary ability both offensively\n\tand defensively.<\/p>\n

          Con<\/span>: Important for overcoming your low hit\n\tdie and for your poor Fortitude saves.<\/p>\n

          Int<\/span>: Only important for skill ranks.<\/p>\n

          Wis<\/span>: Only useful for Will saves, and you can\n\tovercome a penalty with a cloak.<\/p>\n

          Cha<\/span>: Fuels your conversational skills and\n\tyour Ki Pool.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
