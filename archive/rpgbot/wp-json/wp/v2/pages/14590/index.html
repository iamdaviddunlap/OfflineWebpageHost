Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

The Swarmkeeper Ranger is designed to add a bit of control effects to a Ranger with a mixture of forced movement effects and area control spells. While it\u2019s not going to do the most damage of any Ranger subclass, Swarmkeeper gives options for what to do each turn when attacking. This allows for some very tactical decision making in combat and if that appeals to you, read on.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

If I may draw a comparison from an older edition, the features and expanded spells of Swarmkeeper remind me of the Seeker class from D&D 4E. With great effects like forced movement, difficult terrain, and the restrained condition, while the Swarmkeeper will never be as prolific a controller as a Wizard, an extra half-controller on the field is a great addition to any party.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

For our example build, I Got a Rock, we\u2019re stretching the idea of a swarm of things to include space rocks. Like a meteor swarm. Because we\u2019re using the magic stone cantrip to launch rocks at people in order to be a Wisdom-based Ranger, this allows us to keep our spell save DCs higher than most Rangers so we can take advantage of the great expanded spells from Swarmkeeper, including Web, the best 2nd-level spell in the game.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

                Swarmkeeper Ability Scores<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
                <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

                We have some options as a Swarmkeeper. We can go the traditional route of high Dexterity ranged attacking or Strength based melee combat, but we have some features that call for our spell save DC, so there is a case to be made for being Wisdom based with Druidic Warrior.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

                Str<\/span>: You can maybe start doing some Polearm Master shenanigans or Great Weapon Master, but I don\u2019t recommend that.<\/p>\n

                Dex<\/span>: Use this for AC and ranged attacks.The good choice.<\/p>\n

                Con<\/span>: HP and Concentration saves. Good for us.<\/p>\n

                Int<\/span>: Let someone else handle the Scholar checks.<\/p>\n

                Wis<\/span>: We have some great abilities that rely on our Spell DC, so getting this up is a good plan.<\/p>\n

                Cha<\/span>: Let someone else handle the Face checks.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
