Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

War Magic. Huh. Yeah! What is it good for? Absolutely everything a Wizard needs. Durability? We got it. Always going first to instantly win every combat encounter in one spell? We got that too. The War Magic Wizard is a flexible chassis that supports whatever you want to do as a traditional Wizard.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n\n

Compared to the other Wizard Traditions, War Magic is the generalist. The Spell School Traditions each dig down to find something special for one school of magic, while the Bladesingers and Scribes attempt more esoteric ways of Wizardry, but War Magic focuses on making sure that in the middle of a fight, the Wizard can actually do Wizard things; mostly through a combination of enhanced defenses and boosted Initiative.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

        4. Durable Magic<\/span>: If you’re not maintaining\n\t\tConcentration on a spell, you should be casting a spell that requires\n\t\tConcentration.
