Introduction<\/h2>\n\n\n

Ranger spells are an interesting mix of buffs and utility options with a\n\thandful of direct damage spells and healing options. Most of the spells are\n\ttaken from the Druid’s spell list, though the Ranger gets several unique\n\toptions like Hunter’s Mark, and in many cases spells shared with the druid are\n\tthe Ranger’s worst options while spells unique to the Ranger are their best\n\toptions.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n

The Ranger’s spell selection is also limited heavily by WotC’s concepts about\n\twhat a ranger is and where they go on adventures. Spells will heavily favor\n\tfighting in forests (or in forest-like environments like orchards. <\/div>\n\n\n\n\n
