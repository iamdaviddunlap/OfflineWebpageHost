The Rogue got some fun new goodies in the 2024 rules, and their subclasses got a lot of quality of life improvements. The Arcane Trickster is free of spell school restrictions dating back to at least 3 edition, the Assassin is a much more reliable at the cost of its ability to auto-crit, and the Thief got a significant buff to their ability to use magic items. The Soulknife is basically unchanged, though they can now use their psionic knives for Opportunity Attacks, addressing a long-standing pain point.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
